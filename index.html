Privacy Policy — Geomantia Hermetica

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Geomantia Hermetica is a traditional geomantic calculator app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Geomantia Hermetica does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations are performed locally on your device. No user account is required.

Internet Access

The app requests internet permission solely to load local assets within the WebView. No data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
